Product Design

Product Concept
It's a vegan brand, but it differentiates itself with unique colors and designs instead of using green and earth tones, with the concept of a distinctive vegan cosmetic brand. The label is designed with vibrant colors to reflect the product's fragrance, creating a visually appealing aesthetic.

Moisture Cream Label Design
Designed for daily use, it blends colors that evoke a refreshing floral scent. Additionally, the brand logo and vegan logo are positioned at the bottom to represent the brand identity.

Sleeping Mask Label Design
Designed for moisturization before going to bed. It blends warm and rich colors.

Promotional Banner

To express the identity of the vegan brand, the main slogan is "The Vegan Vogue: A Sustainable Soul." The design includes a variety of colors to match plAna's unique brand image,creating a lively and warm atmosphere. By using soap bubbles as the background image, the goal is to radiate a stylish and youthful vibe, distinguishing it from the typical natural vegan look and traditional vegan cosmetics.
